Welcome

Welcome to my website. This has been created as a project for the purpose of logging Placement logs and to demonstrate what we have learned throughout our qualification. It uses a mix of Material Design, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, JavaScript and jQuery to give you a more user friendly experience, but also keeping it simple and making sure that all functions are easily accessible and displayed. Scroll down to see more about me, and to begin creating your own logs. For your benefit, we have put the contents table for your logs, both, at the bottom of the home page and the logs page.

Hi, my names Aaron and this is my project website. I am currently a third year student at Ulster University (Coleraine Campus) studying BSc (Hons) Computing with Business. During this year, I am undergoing work placement at ISArc, a Coleraine based software company, where we are being trained for the first 8 weeks by NowThis to acheive a Level 3 RQF in Web Design and Development, then begin working on Information Systems for the company. I have always been keen to create and design functioning websites ever since my school years, flourishing my interest to pursue a career in this industry. Since I began university, I have acquired knowledge in various programming languages, such as C++ or Java, but also building on my web design skillset in HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP and jQuery.

My aim for this website is to have fresh, modern aesthetics, but also keeping the simplicity throughout the website so that there is no clutter or unnecessary content taking up your screen. It aims to provide the necessary functions needed, but to have it all just one click away.
